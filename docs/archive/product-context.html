Product Context

Problem Statement

UDance addresses the fragmented landscape of dance discovery and studio management in Buenos Aires. Currently, dance enthusiasts struggle to find classes suited to their style and level, while studios lack unified tools for student management, instructor coordination, and event monetization. The platform bridges this gap by providing AI-powered discovery for students and comprehensive management tools for the dance business ecosystem.
